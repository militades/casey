Strapato Ray

Overview

The Strapato Stingrays are an exotic speices of
stingrays that are on the verge of extinction. The
Strapato Ray is one of the endangeredspecies that
belong to the family of the Flying Tree Stingray.
This particular species can only be found on the
western most forest of western Washington.
There are currently about 20 of these rays in
existence and are one of the most protected
species in the world.

Physical Features

  • Brownish Rough skin
  • Sizes range from 1 foot to about 15 feet in diameter
  • Eyes resemble that of an owl and changes color based on temperment
  • Ability to change color and camouflage
  • Males can be identified by a single Stinger and are smaller in size
  • Females have two stingers and larger in size
